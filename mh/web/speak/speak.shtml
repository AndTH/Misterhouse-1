MisterHouse the DJ

If you have a shoutcast compatable mp3 player (e.g. Winamp or Xmms), then click here to listen to what the house is saying, with about a 10 second lag. The 'House DJ' will make random comments once a minute. Background music is usually home grown Bruce Songs. You can also monitor the shoutcast server status here. The default voice is currently Rich16 from AT&T Natural Voices TTS engine. Another compatable TTS engine some great voices can be found at Cepstral.com.

Feel free to read a deep thought, or a tagline, review what was recently spoken, or type something in below.
